Trackers

It can be helpful to keep a food diary of some sorts to keep track of what is going in. Its easy to not realize the amount we consume, whether its too much or too little. If you are interested in keeping some sort of food diary there are plenty of different options.

  • One option is a written diary.
    • Pros: Keeps track of your food choices, can decide whether or not you want to write calories.
    • Cons: Time consuming, calorie calculations inaccurate.
