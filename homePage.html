Make a Change

Have you ever thought, why dont all these diets work for me?

Its about LIFESTYLE changes!

Whats the difference?

Diets are TEMPORARY life changes in order to achieve a result. In reality, weight loss is a slow process and there is no QUICK solution. Without making lifestyle changes weight is bound to end up being gained back. Healthy PERMANENT lifestyle changes are what allows your body to thrive in a healthy, productive way. Looking better and feeling better go hand in hand.

An overwhelming fact is these diets are a lot of the times under researched, so you dont exactly know how healthy or benefical these fad diets are. Still, despite the evidence and advice most nutritionists would give, people still flock to fad diets in order to quickly drop some lbs. Therefore its important to desifer the facts from the fictions.

This website provides information on a number of different diets and the pros and cons of each of these diets. Remember, research on nutrition can be quite subjective based on who is funding the research and/or conducting the experiment. Everyones body is different. Therefore, I wont include benefits that are not related to weight control or loss. Also be aware if I say a diet is miserable that IS my opinion. It is not a fact. The facts represented and mentioned about food groups and their benefits are researched and TRUE FACTS. If you have ever read about a diet backed by research to increase lifespan understand that they only MIGHT be facts. Its impossible to say for sure with fad diets that are underresearched or there is researcher bias.

Losing weight is difficult for most people. This website exists to inspire LIFESTYLE changes.
